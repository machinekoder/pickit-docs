Environmental and operating conditions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

-  Temperature: 5ºC to 40ºC
-  Vibrations: Operating, 2 Grms, 5-500 Hz, 3 axes
-  IP rating: IP55
-  Power supply: USB3 5VDC
-  Humidity: ~95% @ 40°C (non-condensing)
